For Donors

Giving Should Be Helpful, Not Careless

Donations can support daily meals, school needs, hygiene items, maintenance, utilities, and welfare activities. However, every donor should confirm current needs and official donation channels before making a contribution.

This page is written to guide potential donors responsibly. Bank details, account names, contact numbers, and urgent needs must be verified directly with Rumah Anak Yatim / Rumah Amal Raudhatul Jannah Banting before the final website is published.

Ways To Help

Donation Categories

Food And Groceries

Rice, cooking oil, canned food, biscuits, milk, eggs, fresh ingredients, and other kitchen essentials.

Education Support

School bags, stationery, books, printing supplies, uniforms, and learning materials.

Hygiene Essentials

Toiletries, detergent, cleaning supplies, towels, tissues, and personal care items.

Welfare And Utilities

Electricity, water, maintenance, transport, medical needs, and general home operations.

Donation Instructions

Before Making A Donation

  1. Contact the organization to confirm current needs.
  2. Ask for the official donation method or bank account details.
  3. Prepare items in clean, usable, and suitable condition.
  4. Arrange a suitable delivery or visit time before coming.
  5. Keep proof of donation or transfer for personal records.

Volunteer Support

Other Ways To Contribute

Support does not always need to be financial. Volunteers may assist through approved programs such as tutoring, cleaning activities, event support, motivational sessions, or skills-based help.

Group visits should be arranged properly. Turning up without notice may disturb the home schedule, so visitors should contact the organization first.

Contact Before Visiting

Responsible Giving

Donor Checklist

  • Confirm the home accepts the items you want to donate.
  • Do not donate expired food, damaged clothing, or unusable goods.
  • Respect children's privacy when taking photos.
  • Use verified contact details and official donation channels.
  • Ask permission before publishing visit photos online.
