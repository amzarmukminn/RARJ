Background

About The Home

Rumah Anak Yatim / Rumah Amal Raudhatul Jannah Banting is represented in this learning website as a welfare organization, meaning a group that supports community wellbeing through care, assistance, and service. It cares for children and supports their daily development through shelter, guidance, education, and community assistance.

Purpose

Mission And Objectives

Mission

To provide a caring and supportive environment where children can receive shelter, education support, moral guidance, and encouragement for a better future.

Vision

To become a trusted community-supported home that helps children grow with dignity, confidence, and hope.

Values

Care, responsibility, trust, respect, education, and sincere community cooperation.

Website Objectives
