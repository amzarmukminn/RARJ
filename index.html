Welcome

Helping Visitors And Donors Understand The Home

Welcome to this learning website for Rumah Amal Raudhatul Jannah. This website introduces the home to potential donors, visitors, and volunteers by presenting its background, activity highlights, donation guidance, and contact information in a clear and responsible way.

The website is designed to help the public understand how they may support children’s welfare through useful contributions, planned visits, and respectful community involvement. All final contact and donation details should be verified directly with the organization before publication.
