Welcome

Helping Visitors And Donors Understand The Home

This learning website introduces Rumah Anak Yatim / Rumah Amal Raudhatul Jannah Banting to potential donors, visitors, and volunteers. It highlights the home background, community activities, donation guidance, and contact information in a clear and responsible way.

Final facts such as address, contact number, donation account, and current needs must be confirmed directly with the organization before publication.
